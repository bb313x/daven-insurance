Our Story

Built to Protect Small Business Owners

InsureMyBiz123 was founded by Bryan and Kirk, two entrepreneurs who saw a gap in the market. Small business owners in New York, New Jersey, and Florida were either getting overcharged by big carriers or ignored by agents who didn't understand their specific needs.

We decided to do something different. We got our licenses. We built relationships with top-tier carriers. And we made it our mission to be the trusted advisor that every business owner deserves.

Today, we serve hundreds of small businesses — from construction firms and restaurants to professional services and retail shops. Every single client gets personalized attention and coverage that actually fits their business.
